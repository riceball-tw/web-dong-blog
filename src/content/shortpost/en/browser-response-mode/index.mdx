The most noticeable shortcuts in frontend are definitely pressed hundreds of times a day with these two buttons!

- Inspect page elements: Ctrl + Shift + C
- Device size preview: Ctrl + Shift + M

Some browsers may require opening DevTools (F12) for them to take effect.
