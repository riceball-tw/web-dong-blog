1. Write down the complete picture of the problem and prepare to message your colleague.
2. Press the "Submit" button.
3. Wait for solutions to automatically emerge and reply, "It's okay, I found a solution."

Writing down the problem in detail is actually part of the debugging process; this time, your colleague is the rubber duck (rubber duck debugging). As for how to go from defining the problem to generating solutions, you can refer to a previous article I wrote: [Goal, Problem, Solution](https://www.webdong.dev/post/background-problem-solution/).
