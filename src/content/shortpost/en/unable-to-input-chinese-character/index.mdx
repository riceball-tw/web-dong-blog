headline: Linux Snap (VSCode) Cannot Input Chinese Characters
publishDate: 2025-03-19
category: Technical Discussion
---

Recently, I refurbished my computer and installed Ubuntu for daily use, and used App Center ([Snap](https://ivonblog.com/posts/linux-snap-pros-and-cons/)) to install commonly used apps like VSCode. Later, I realized that I couldn't input Chinese characters correctly...

Due to its unique sandbox mechanism, its apps cannot properly access system settings. It seems that a complete rebuild is necessary to resolve this issue, which might explain why the [related issue](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/96041) discussion for VSCode has been ongoing since 2020 and remains unresolved. Firefox and Discord do not have this problem.

Additionally, trying out the [Zed editor](https://zed.dev/) also encountered issues with inputting Chinese characters, yet another pit to fill...
